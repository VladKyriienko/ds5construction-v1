Our Services

At DS5 Construction Ltd, we deliver residential and commercial construction services across London, managing projects from initial planning through to completion. Our work is based on practical site experience, technical coordination and consistent delivery at every stage.

House Renovation

A house renovation is more than a visual upgrade. It is a coordinated process of improving the layout, replacing outdated elements and bringing the property up to a higher technical and practical standard. At DS5 Construction Ltd, we deliver full and partial house renovations across London, helping clients transform tired or inefficient properties into well-planned homes suited to modern living.

House Extension

A well-designed house extension should feel fully integrated with the existing property rather than added on as a separate structure. At DS5 Construction Ltd, we build extensions that increase usable space, improve natural light, and enhance how the home functions day to day.

Summer House

A summer house or garden room should be built as a properly insulated structure rather than treated as a simple seasonal outbuilding. At DS5 Construction Ltd, we design and build bespoke garden buildings for use as home offices, leisure spaces, guest accommodation, or as flexible home extensions.

New Build

A new build project requires disciplined coordination from initial planning through to final handover. At DS5 Construction Ltd, we deliver new build projects across London, ensuring that the structure, building envelope, services and finishes are aligned from the outset and delivered in line with your brief, programme and quality expectations.

Kitchen

A kitchen refurbishment should balance appearance, functionality and technical coordination. Bathroom
