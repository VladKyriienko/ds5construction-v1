House Renovation

A house renovation is more than a visual upgrade. It is a coordinated process of improving the layout, replacing outdated elements and bringing the property up to a higher technical and practical standard. At DS5 Construction Ltd, we deliver full and partial house renovations across London, helping clients transform tired or inefficient properties into well-planned homes suited to modern living.
Many renovation projects involve opening up enclosed rooms, upgrading ageing services, improving the flow between spaces and raising the overall standard of finish. Our role is to manage these works as one coordinated build, ensuring that the structure, services and finishes are properly integrated throughout the project.

Renovation Options

  • Indoor Renovations
    Focused on internal upgrades such as kitchen refurbishment and bathroom renovation, improving day-to-day use, layout and finish quality.
  • Loft and Basement Conversions
    A practical way to unlock underused space within the existing building and increase the property's functionality and value.
  • House Expansions
    Suitable where additional living space is needed to support changing household requirements.
  • Property Refurbishment
    A broader upgrade focused on improving the property's overall condition, appearance, and usability.
  • Flooring and Roofing Works
    Includes flooring installation, roofing and guttering works to improve durability, weather protection and the overall standard of finish.
  • Essential Installations
    Covers plumbing, heating and core service upgrades that are essential for comfort, safety and reliable long-term performance.

What the service includes

Our house renovation works typically include strip-out, internal demolition, structural alterations, wall reconfiguration, first- and second-fix electrics, plumbing, heating, plastering, flooring, decorating, kitchens, bathrooms, and bespoke joinery. Where required, works are carried out in line with current Building Regulations and project-specific technical requirements.

How we deliver it

Every renovation begins with an assessment of how the property currently functions and how it needs to perform once completed. We then sequence demolition, structural works, service installations and final finishes in the correct order, so that each stage supports the next and the overall build remains organised, efficient and properly controlled.

The result

The finished home is better organised, better performing and better suited to modern living, with improved comfort, a stronger layout and a cleaner overall finish.
