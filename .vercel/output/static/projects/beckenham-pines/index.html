The Pines, Beckenham

Full Apartment Renovation

This project involved the full renovation of a third-floor apartment, with a complete upgrade of the interior, building services and overall layout. Our team carried out a full strip-out of the existing finishes, flooring, kitchen and part of the internal partitioning, after which the apartment was fully renovated in line with the client’s brief.
One of the key elements of the project was the reconfiguration of the kitchen, which included removing the wall between the kitchen and dining area and installing a structural steel beam in accordance with the engineering drawings. This allowed the space to be opened up and reorganised into a more practical and modern layout.

Scope of Works

Strip-Out and Preparation

  • Removal of the existing kitchen and flooring
  • Demolition of the wall between the kitchen and dining area
  • Removal of old internal finishes
  • Waste removal and site clearance

New Building Services

  • Full electrical rewire throughout the apartment
  • New wiring, sockets, switches and lighting
  • New heating system throughout
  • Installation of new radiators and pipework
  • New plumbing and drainage system

Kitchen

  • Installation of a new kitchen
  • Fitting of kitchen units and worktops
  • Appliance connections
  • Tiling above the work surfaces
  • Installation of new kitchen flooring

Bespoke Joinery

  • Full refurbishment of the existing bathroom
  • Installation of new sanitaryware
  • Integration of new plumbing and electrical connections

Bathroom

  • Full renovation of the shower room
  • New sanitaryware
  • Waterproofing works
  • Wall and floor tiling
  • Installation of the shower area, basin and associated fittings

Internal Finishes

  • New flooring throughout the apartment
  • New skirting boards
  • New door linings and doors
  • Plastering works
  • Full decoration throughout

Balcony

  • Replacement of the sliding balcony doors
  • Upgrade and finishing of the balcony area

The result

The result was a fully renovated apartment with upgraded services, a more functional layout, and bespoke joinery, creating a modern, practical living space.
